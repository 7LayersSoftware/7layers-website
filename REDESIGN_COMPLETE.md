## Design System Implemented

### Color Palette
- **Primary Dark**: Midnight Obsidian (#0A1128)
- **Primary Light**: Off-white (#F8F9FA)
- **Accent**: Kinetic Teal (#00F5D4)
- **Cards**: Deep blue (#12182F)
- **Borders**: Subtle borders with 0.5 opacity

### Typography
- **Headlines**: Bold, up to 7xl on hero sections
- **Subheadings**: Large, 2-4xl throughout
- **Body**: Clean, 16-18px with 1.6 line-height
- **Labels**: Small caps, uppercase tracking

### Spacing & Layout
- **Mobile-first** responsive design
- **Container**: Max-width with consistent padding
- **Grid gaps**: 8-12 units throughout
- **Section spacing**: 20-32 units (py-20 to py-32)

### Animations & Interactions
- **Hover effects**: Smooth 300-500ms transitions
- **Scale transforms**: Subtle -translate-y-1 to -translate-y-2
- **Color transitions**: Gradient and opacity changes
- **Arrow animations**: Translate with rotation effects
- **Scroll indicators**: Bounce animations
